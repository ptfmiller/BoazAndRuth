Harvest Thrift

Gently Used Furniture & Clothing 

An abundance of gently used clothing, including our new Designer Rack with top name brands, along with home furnishings and accessories. We have great bargains, and receive new arrivals daily. Come shop and find your unique treasure today!

Every purchase and donation you make at Harvest Thrift helps rebuild lives and the community of Highland Park. Your patronage and generosity helps provide housing, life skills and employment for those transitioning to a better way of life. For shopping AND donations to our Thrift Stores, please visit soon!
